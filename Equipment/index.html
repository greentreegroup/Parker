Our Financing Service

Equipment needed to run a business can be both large in scale, and expensive. However, the equipment is necessary to perform successful work for customers.

Equipment financing helps business owners get funding to purchase or lease equipment without depleting their working capital. Equipment loans can vary from lender to lender.

That’s why it’s important to partner with a financing company that can offer you the best options to fit your budget and needs.

Benefits of Equipment Financing

Benefits of leasing far outweigh other funding options for new equipment.

Below is a list of perks that come with leasing equipment.


  • With equipment loans or leases, the equipment you’re getting serves as your collateral, which means you can often get good rates on these loans.


  • Operating leases are a tax-deductible overhead expense, meaning you can deduct the lease payments from your corporate income.


  • Equipment does not have to be depreciated over five to seven years.


  • Not considered a long-term debt or liability. Makes you more attractive to traditional lenders when you need them.


  • Little downpayment. Since a lease does not require a down payment, it is equivalent to 100 percent financing.


  • As your business grows and your needs change, you can add or upgrade at any point during the lease term through add-on or master leases.


  • If the nature of your industry demands that you have the latest technology, a short-term operating lease can help you get the equipment and keep your cash.


  • Lessor assumes and manages the risk of equipment ownership. At the end of the lease, the lessor is responsible for the disposition of the asset.
